Towcester Cycling Club

Who We Are.

We are a local family friendly cycling club based in Towcester. We organise group rides on the weekends and we also train at various times during the week, mostly in the evenings. We train for various occassions, be it for up coming races/ competetive events or to help with riding and group riding in general.

We are a welcoming, family friendly cycling club. Everyone is here for the love of cycling and as a small community we enjoy the sport together. Anyone, any age and all ability's are welcome to join our cycling club. Everyone is here to help each other, so if you need any advice or just fancy a social ride then get involved. For training/ group rides we post times and dates on the schedule right here on the website. We have an annual charge of £180 to join the club. If you would like to join us, please contact us via the contact page.

Compete.

We do take part in local, national and even international events. We train weekly for this and for anyone interested in competing and representing Towcester CC please register your interest with the admins at race@towcestercc.co.uk there is an additional monthly fee of £10 for this which is to cover training/ racing jerseys, water bottles, general maintenance of your bikes, it also contributes towards travel to local events, snacks and refreshments at events.

The race membership also gives you access to the weekly race training meets and a further 10% discount at the club for merchandise. For National and international events there is additional charges based on the event and the location (hotel stay/ travel/ bike transport).

Coffee & Cake.

As much as we love to race, we also love coffee and cake we are not just about the racing, we love cycling, and there is nothing more satisfying than a nice cup of coffee and a slice of cake after a nice social ride with our friends at the club. Racing isn't our only focus at Towcester CC, we have weekly casual rides in large and small groups.

We also organise trips domestically and abroad, so if you'd like to get away for a long weekend in the lake district or even the south of France, with your bike and a friendly group you're in the right place. We post up coming trips for both social and race events on the site and on our socials, so keep an eye out for those. For now here are some pictures from previous trips.

Explore.

We orgainse bi-monthly trips for club members, this is usually within the UK and depending minimum numbers being met. We also have an annual trip to europe, again depending on availability and minimum numbers being met. The annual trip is usually either a long weekend (fri morning to monday night) or a week.

We post a shortlist of locations on the website in January for the annual trip in July, club members then have until February to vote for destination. Once the destination has been decided we start taking deposits and organising the trip. If you'd like to join us on our next annual trip or even our bi-monthly domestic trips, keep an eye out on the website, socials and your email for more information.
