Towcester Cycling Club

Who We Are.

We are a local family friendly cycling club based in Towcester. We organise group rides on the weekends and we also train at various times during the week, mostly in the evenings. We train for various occassions, be it for up coming races/ competetive events or to help with riding and group riding in general. We are welcome, family friendly cycling club.
Everyone is here for the love of cycling and as small community we enjoy the sport together. Anyone, any age and all ability's are welcome to join our cycling club. Everyone is here to help each other, so if you need any advice or just fancy a social ride then get involved. For training/ group rides we post times and dates on the schedule right here on the website. We have an annual charge of £180 to join the club. If you would like to join us, please contact us via the contact page.

Compete.

We do take part in local, national and even international events. We train weekly for this and for anyone interested in competing and representing Towcester CC please register your interest with the admins at race@towcestercc.co.uk there is an additional monthly fee of £10 for this which is to cover training/ racing jerseys, water bottles, general maintenance of your bikes, it also contributes towards travel to local events, snacks and refreshments at events. The race membership also gives you access to the weekly race training meets and a further 10% discount at the club for merchandise. For National and international events there is additional charges based on the event and the location (hotel stay/ travel/ bike transport).
